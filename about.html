About Me

Hi! I’m a Computer Science student at CIIT College of Arts and Technology with a strong passion for web development, system administration, and server technologies. I enjoy building and managing digital solutions that combine performance, security, and scalability.

I’m also deeply interested in creating game servers, deploying Dockerized applications, and self-hosting various services . My long-term goal is to build my own game server hosting company that provides low-latency connections, fast and powerful servers, and affordable hosting solutions for gamers and developers alike.

Before college, I studied at STI College Tagaytay, under the Mobile App and Web Development (ICT) strand, where I developed a solid foundation in programming and web technologies. I also had the opportunity to participate in local and cluster-level coding competitions, which helped me sharpen my problem-solving and teamwork skills.

I’m always eager to learn, explore new technologies, and take on challenges that push my skills further—especially in the fields of server management, containerization, and web infrastructure.
